Makkari is a lightweight PHP framework designed to help students understand the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architectural pattern. By providing a simple and efficient structure for building web applications, Makkari makes it easy for students to grasp the organization of code and the separation of concerns inherent in MVC. The framework is tailored to be beginner-friendly, allowing students to quickly learn and apply core concepts without getting overwhelmed by the complexity of more advanced PHP frameworks. Makkari serves as an excellent starting point for students embarking on web application development, equipping them with the foundational knowledge and skills needed to confidently work with larger frameworks in the future.
